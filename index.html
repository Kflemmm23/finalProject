Greenhouse Gases

Animal agriculture is responsible for 51% of greenhouse gas emissions, more than the combined exhaust from all transportation (road, rail, air and marine) at 13%.*

Land

Covering 45% of the earthâ€™s total land, animal agriculture is the leading cause of species extinction, ocean dead zones, water pollution, and habitat destruction.*

Water

5% of water consumed in the US is used by private homes. 55% of water consumed in the US is for animal agriculture.*

Ocean Dead Zones

Livestock operations on land have created more than 500 nitrogen-flooded ocean deadzones around the world.*

Deforestation

Animal agriculture is responsible for up to 91% of Amazon destruction and is the leading cause of biodiversity loss.*

What Can we Do?



Animal agriculture runs on supply and demand.

We can make a difference by eating less animal products.
